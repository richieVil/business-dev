### 1.1 Mission Statement
To eliminate the "Fiscalization Gap" for Mexican SMBs by providing a high-speed, asynchronous bridge between modern POS payloads and the SAT regulatory environment.

"A fiscal middleware bridge that converts POS transaction payloads into SAT-compliant invoices for Mexican SMBs via a WhatsApp Identity Network to eliminate checkout bottlenecks."

| **Gains** | Desired outcomes. | A single "Inbox" for all fiscal deductions across all stores. |

### 3.2 The "Micro-Moment" of Pain
**The Scenario:** A customer finishes a purchase at a boutique shop. They ask for a factura.
**The Reality:** The seller says, *"Send an email with your data and a photo of the ticket,"* or *"Go to our website and type in this 20-digit code."* 
**The Friction:** The customer gets home, forgets the ticket, or tries the website and it fails to load on their phone. The business looks unprofessional, and the customer loses their tax deduction. If the seller tries to "auto-sign" at the POS, the transaction takes 3x longer, frustrating everyone in line.

## 4. The Value Map (Internal Solution)

### 4.1 Pain Relievers
*   **Asynchronous Bridge:** The cashier prints a short-code; the buyer fulfills on WhatsApp.
*   **Email-Bypass:** No more "Sending info to a black hole." The bot confirms receipt and issues the document instantly.
*   **PDF Parsing:** Bot accepts direct PDF uploads of the "Constancia de Situación Fiscal" for 100% data accuracy (Zero manual entry).

### 4.2 Gain Creators
*   **The Identity Vault:** The "Fiscal Handshake" is done once. If Juan uploads his Constancia PDF to the Fiscal-Bridge number at a pharmacy, the bot **already knows him** when he scans a code at the hardware store.
*   **One-Click Deductibility:** Buyers receive their XML/PDF instantly via WhatsApp, ensuring they never lose a tax deduction.
