## 2. The Master Risk Ledger (Pre-Mortem)

| Risk Type | Failure Scenario | Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Platform Risk** | Meta bans the central WhatsApp number for "Spam." | **Multi-WABA Balancing:** Distribute sellers across 5+ API accounts; enforce strict "Service-Only" templates. |
| **Security Risk** | CSD (Digital Seals) are leaked or compromised. | **Zero-Knowledge Vault:** Use HSM (Hardware Security Modules) or KMS where keys are never stored in memory as plain text. |
| **UX Risk** | Chat thread becomes cluttered with 20 different store receipts. | **Structured Searchability:** Force `#BrandName` prefixes; Provide a "Magic Link" dashboard for historical lookups. |
| **Brand Risk** | High-end sellers feel the bot is too "generic." | **Contextual Wrapping:** Dynamic bot headers/logos that "skin" the chat based on the SellerID. |
| **Data Risk** | SAT scrutinizes us as a "Data Aggregator." | **Ephemeral Storage:** Purge transaction payloads within 72 hours; retain only the Identity Vault (RFC metadata). |
| **Churn Risk** | Large Enterprise users build their own version. | **Tiered Autonomy:** Allow them to "Bring Your Own WABA/PAC" while retaining our Catalog/Identity logic. |

## 3. Strategic Evolution (Pivot Scenarios)

1.  **The Customer Up-Level:** Pivot from SMB Retail to **Franchise Groups** or **Malls**. Sell the "Unified Fiscal Branding" to the Mall Manager as a perk for all tenants.
2.  **The Business Model Flip:** Pivot from SaaS to **Lending Brokerage**. Give the invoicing away for free and monetize the "CFO Data" by facilitating working-capital loans for the businesses (The "CFO Agent" tie-in).
3.  **The Adjacent Problem:** Pivot from "Facturas" to **"Employee Deductions"**. Use the Identity Vault to help employees at Maquiladoras automatically capture their grocery/fuel receipts for tax-exempt "Social Welfare" funds.
